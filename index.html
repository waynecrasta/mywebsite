About Me

I am currently a sophomore majoring in Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign. I am actively seeking a internship in software or hardware development for the summer of 2015.

I was born in Dubai, UAE, moved to Mississauga, Canada in the summer of 2002, and then moved to New Jersey in the summer of 2005. Before attending UIUC, I attended Morris Catholic High School in Denville, NJ.

I chose to attend UIUC because I knew it would make me a well-rounded engineer. For example, most schools do not require you to take Physics classes for Computer Engineering/Science but UIUC requires all engineers to take Mechanics, E&M, Quantum, and Thermal Physics. At the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, I have taken multiple ECE classes, in which I have worked with Arduinos, UNIX Command line, CAD software, VHDL, circuits, HTML, C, C++, Python, and LC-3 Assembly Language. These classes are ECE 110, ECE 120, and ECE 220.
